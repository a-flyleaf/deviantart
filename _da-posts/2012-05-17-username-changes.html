--- title: Username Changes date: 2012-05-17 14:10 footnote: comment: Honestly I just think this is funny in retrospect because 1) now I've changed my dA username (mostly to take advantage of the opportunity while I had it, but really wouldn't have minded still having the old one) and 2) *oh you sweet summer child*, I had yet to experience the broken link hellpit that was Tumblr urls. date: 2022-09-20 --- So apparently, now people can change their username... if and only if they have a premium membership. (For the record, SimbaTheHuman/thefrozentundra and I (mostly Tundra) totally called it. )

Honestly, I think people are jumping on this bandwagon way too quickly. I can understand if you've been wanting to change your dA name for a while, but I've seen people changing their names because "this is a cool new feature and I want to try it" or "I've already changed accounts a bunch of times but now I don't have to anymore" and those are both crappy reasons. (I mean, I've always been kinda biased against people who change their accounts once every twenty days or whatever, but that's another rant...).

Seriously. Put some thought into it before you change your name. Yes, I know you can change your name again in another six months... but you can't go back to the original. Waste of usernames much? In a few years from now we'll have some people with notes on their pages that say "Formerly kawaiidesugal9000, warriorz4lief, pokeymanzrule, starlightmoonwolf51..." etc. (Forget the fact those are all crappy usernames and someone who wants to use them should really think twice; that's just sarcasm.) Point is, do you really need to be changing your username so often?

Yeah, I know I'm ranting, and I don't know why you're still reading this. I'm just pretty darned sick of seeing a bunch of messages in my Inbox saying that people I watch just changed their username, usually to something worse than the original. Do I use thousands and thousands of names on the Internet? Yeah. Do I need to be changing my dA name every six months to reflect my current most-frequently-used name? Heck no.

(And for those who are wondering: No, I'm not just saying this because I don't have a premium membership. Screw premiums; I can put up with a few ads and no journal skins/polls/etc. I'd still be complaining about this sort of thing nonetheless.)
